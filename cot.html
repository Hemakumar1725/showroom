Wooden Cot's

Wooden cots come in single, double, and queen sizes.

Single cots are good for small spaces and can be used for newborns.

Double cots can fit one adult or a couple of young children.


Solid wood cots are made from heavy, sturdy woods like teak or sheesham.

They are durable and can last a long time. Solid wood cots are also less likely

to contain harmful chemicals than cots made from composite materials.
